We know Fibreglass.

Architecture

We specialise in Dome Roofing, with projects completed for Hervey Bay RSL, Catholic Church Brisbane, Mosque Brisbane and Sikh Temple Melbourne. We also have experience across building, construction and architecture including:

  • Fraser Coast Regional Council: Boardwalk design
  • Hervey Bay Aquatic Centre: Sculptures and Interactive Play Elements

Vehicle Customisation

We have completed numerous projects for different types of heavy vehicles. We can support specific requirements for appearance, aerodynamics, durability and weight. Previous projects include:

  • Unimog Expedition: Customised vehicle auxiliaries; insulated cabin construction, body and cab modifications
  • Ergon Energy Elevating work platform fibreglass maintenance and repairs.
  • FGS Composites Work Van: CAD designed modification plans, custom built living quarters, body and cab modifications.

Mining

Fibreglass has excellent corrosion resistant properties resulting in a extensive use in the mining industry. We have delivered construction and maintenance projects in Australia and overseas.

Newcrest Mining:
  • Refurbish and replacement of FRP pipelines, scrubbers, grating.
  • Fabrication of conveyor stacker structural support and conveyor belt tensioners

Mass Transport

Fibreglass composites are used widely in mass transit vehicle components. We produce both interior and exterior fibreglass parts for new construction and retrofits. Past projects include:

  • Downer EDI Rail and Bombardier: Static and live simulator manufacturing and installation. Prototyping of train interior and locomotive fronts. Repair and modification of train passenger car FRP components.
  • Queensland Rail, Aurizon: Locomotive soundproofing, panel design and production.
  • Futuris: Train passenger seat production and modification.
